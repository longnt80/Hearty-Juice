About Hearty Juice

It’s Easy Being Green!

Unlike many other juice brands, Hearty Juice is not a fruit juice – it’s a green juice. Specially Condensation Juice.

So what’s the difference?

Fruits naturally contain a type of sugar called fructose – so if your juice contains too many fruits, the chances are its sugar levels will be tipping the high side of the scales too.

And we all know too much sugar is not a good thing for our health…

Green juice, on the other hand, is made primarily from organic green vegetables – yummy, healthy things like spinach, kale, celery, asparagus, and broccoli. Sure, you can throw in an apple, a kiwi or a handful of berries for some fruity flavour, but your veggie-based green juice will remain reassuringly low in sugar.

Sounds great - but I’m not sure about the colour…

If downing a glass of the green stuff doesn’t get your ‘juices’ flowing, you’re not alone! In fact, a recent survey found that 28% of adults actually “feared” the look of green juice, despite knowing it was the healthiest option. All we can say to that is, don’t judge a green juice by its colour!

With an array of awesome flavor combinations – from milder-tasting veggies like celery and cucumber, through to stronger greens such as kale and dandelion, there’s a delicious green juice recipe for everyone.

‘Green’ on green juice?

If you’re a novice to the world of green juice, just let our friendly Hearty Juice staff know – they’ll be happy to advise on the best flavours for your palate, and can even design a custom green juice recipe just for you.
